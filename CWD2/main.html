About Us

Many children around the world doesn't have the chance to enjoy life of being a kid where they got to recieve gifts like toys, clothes and candies during christmas. Santa Site was build to reach children who are expecting that Santa Claus will give them gifts this coming Christmas. We at Santa Site asks your kind and loving heart to offer some donations for our dear children around the world. And we are hundred percent sure that your kind donations will give a big smile to the face of the children.
